# Response: Handling LegalBot's Contract Review Request (Without TAP Skill)

## Situation Assessment

I received a message from LegalBot asking me to review specific contract clauses -- liability limitations and IP assignment terms in section 4.2 of a partnership agreement with Acme Corp. I am a general-purpose assistant agent without legal expertise. I do not have the domain knowledge required to competently evaluate contract terms, liability clauses, or IP assignment provisions.

## Steps I Would Take

### Step 1: Acknowledge the message promptly

I would reply to LegalBot to confirm receipt so they know the message was not lost or ignored.

### Step 2: Clearly state my limitations

I would be transparent that I lack legal expertise and am not qualified to provide a position on contract terms, liability limitations, or IP assignment clauses. Providing uninformed opinions on legal matters could cause real harm.

### Step 3: Escalate to my human operator

This is the most important step. Since I cannot competently handle a legal review, I need to surface this request to my human operator (the person or team who manages me). They can either:

- Review the contract terms themselves,
- Engage a qualified legal professional or legal AI agent, or
- Provide me with specific instructions on how to respond to LegalBot.

### Step 4: Send a reply to LegalBot

I would compose and send a message back through the messaging gateway.

## Exact Message I Would Send to LegalBot

> Hi LegalBot,
>
> Thank you for reaching out about the Acme Corp partnership contract. I've received your message regarding the concerns with section 4.2, specifically the liability limitations and IP assignment terms.
>
> However, I need to be upfront: I'm a general-purpose assistant and I don't have the legal expertise required to meaningfully review or take a position on contract clauses like these. Providing an uninformed opinion on liability and IP terms could lead to poor outcomes, so I don't want to do that.
>
> I'm escalating this to my human operator right now so they can review your concerns and either respond directly or loop in someone with the appropriate legal expertise. I'll follow up once I have guidance.
>
> Please let me know if there's any additional context or urgency I should relay to them.

## Exact Escalation to My Human Operator

I would surface the following to the operator (via whatever notification or escalation mechanism is available to me):

> **Escalation: Legal review request from LegalBot**
>
> LegalBot has sent a message requesting a review of contract terms for a partnership with Acme Corp. Specifically, they have concerns about:
>
> - Liability limitations in section 4.2
> - IP assignment terms in section 4.2
>
> This is outside my capabilities as a general-purpose assistant. I've acknowledged receipt to LegalBot and let them know I'm escalating. Please advise on how to proceed or respond directly to LegalBot.

## What I Would NOT Do

- **I would not attempt to review the contract terms myself.** I lack legal training, and offering amateur legal analysis could expose the operator to real liability or result in unfavorable contract terms being accepted.
- **I would not ignore the message or delay indefinitely.** LegalBot raised specific, actionable concerns that need a timely response.
- **I would not fabricate expertise or hedge with vague legal-sounding language.** This would be misleading and potentially harmful.
- **I would not forward the full contract contents to any third party** without explicit operator authorization, since contract details are typically confidential.

## Summary

The correct course of action is: (1) acknowledge receipt, (2) be honest about my limitations, (3) escalate to the human operator immediately, and (4) inform LegalBot that the matter is being routed to someone who can properly address it. This protects the operator's interests while maintaining trust and responsiveness in the agent-to-agent communication channel.
